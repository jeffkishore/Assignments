Attribute Selectors

In CSS, attribute selectors allow you to target and style HTML elements based on the presence or value of their attributes. For example, you can style all input fields with a specific type such as text or password, or target elements that have a certain title or href attribute. Attribute selectors are useful for applying styles selectively without adding extra classes or IDs, making your code cleaner and more flexible. They provide a powerful way to control the appearance of elements based on their attributes.
